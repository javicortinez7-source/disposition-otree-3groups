Disposition Effect oTree Experiment - 3 Groups

Design:
1. Gain condition: participants see six gain scenarios.
2. Loss condition: participants see six loss scenarios.
3. Mixed/control condition: participants see both gain and loss scenarios.

Hypotheses covered:
H1: Participants in the loss condition are less likely to sell than participants in the gain condition.
H2: Anticipated regret is associated with holding losing investments.
H3: Loss aversion strengthens the tendency to hold losing investments.
H4: Investment experience and confidence affect the strength of the disposition effect.
